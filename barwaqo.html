ABOUT US

our history

Barwako Salt Production and Trade Cooperative was founded in 2021 by Abdirisak Farah Haajir with a mission to bring structure, quality, and opportunity to local salt production. Starting with a small network of producers, the cooperative focused on sustainable harvesting, improving packaging, and expanding trade. Today, Barwako stands as a united force promoting economic growth, fair trade, and traditional excellence.

our mission

Our mission is to produce and supply high-quality, naturally harvested salt while empowering local communities through cooperative trade. We aim to ensure sustainable practices, create jobs, and elevate the standards of the salt industry across all ethiopia region specially Oromia and beyond.

our vission

To become a leading cooperative in salt production and trade in the all Africa—recognized for quality, sustainability, and community impact—while preserving the traditional salt-making heritage for future generations.
